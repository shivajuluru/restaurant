Secret Receipe Of Shiva Restaurant

Shiva Restaurant is a delightful dining destination that caters to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food lovers. Known for its warm ambiance and diverse menu, the restaurant offers a perfect blend of traditional and contemporary flavors

Veg Menu

Indian Classics: Paneer butter masala, dal makhani, palak paneer, and aloo gobhi.

South Indian Favorites: Masala dosa, idli, and vada served with fresh chutneys and sambar.

Chinese-Inspired Dishes: Veg Manchurian, chili paneer, and veg fried rice.

Non Veg Menu

Tandoori Specialties: Juicy chicken tikka, tandoori chicken, and seekh kebabs cooked to perfection in a clay oven.

Curries: Rich and flavorful chicken curry, mutton rogan josh, and butter chicken.

Biryani: Aromatic chicken biryani and mutton biryani, served with raita.

Declaration

Shiva Restaurant proudly serves a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, offering quality, taste, and a welcoming atmosphere for all. Known for its flavorful cuisine and excellent service, it ensures a delightful dining experience for every guest."
